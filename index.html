iPad Setup Instructions for Verkada Cameras

01 Turn on the iPad and you will be greeted by the Hello screen. Swipe up to begin.

02 Follow the on-screen instructions to select your language, country, and other preferences.

03 On the Quick Start screen, select Set Up Manually at the bottom.

04 Choose your Wi-Fi network and log in.

05 When the iPad displays the Remote Management section, ensure it states that the iPad is owned by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. If it does, proceed to enroll the iPad.

06 On the Update Your iPad Automatically screen, press Continue.

07 Once the setup process is complete, you will be prompted to set a passcode. Please create one now.

08 Wait a few minutes for the iPad to display the preconfigured applications, including Settings and the Verkada Desk Station app.

09 Once the Verkada Desk Station app finishes downloading, open it.

10 In the app, select Let's Get Started to set up the iPad as a desk station.

11 In your Verkada Command account, go to Add Devices

In your Verkada Command account, go to Add Devices by selecting the All Products tab on the left side (the icon with 9 dots). The Add Devices option is the plus icon at the bottom right corner of the tab that appears.

12 In the box labeled Enter Serial or Order Number, enter the code provided by your Verkada app.

13 Click the blue Add 1 Device button to the right.

14 You have now added a desk station to your account! Select the desk station you just added (it will say NEW below it) and on the right-hand side, assign it a new name, location, and site.

15 After selecting the necessary desk station and editing the information, click the arrow next to its image on the right-hand side.

16 Select Manage Tiles and choose the camera you need from the tab that opens.

You will be provided with all the camera view options you can include on the iPad and can customize the views as needed.

Questions and Answers

Find quick answers to common questions about managing your Verkada camera system.

I have lots of different camera views, how should I pick which ones will go on the ipad?

To select limited camera views for a security iPad, prioritize high-risk areas like entrances and exits. Include high-traffic zones such as lobbies and hallways. Cover critical operational areas like server rooms and storage. Address any blind spots not visible from other cameras. Consider areas with a history of incidents. Aim for balanced coverage without overwhelming the viewer. Regularly review and update the selected views to ensure they meet current security needs.

How can I set up alerts to be notified when the camera detects security concerns such as group gatherings, cars, tampering, trespassing, etc?

Verkada offers AI features that allow you to customize alerts to specific circumstances and boundaries. These can be set up by going to all products → admin → alerts → new alert
Ex: AI-powered alerts can be set up using natural language to monitor specific events, such as detecting a person not wearing a safety vest or a specific vehicle arriving

How can I give someone access to Verkada command functionalities?

Go to org settings and choose users here you will have the option to add a user by entering their personal information

What if I just want to view all of the cameras for my location through the Verkada command website?

You can do this by going to all products and then cameras. On cameras home it will all be listed on one page though by selecting grid you will be able to have a customized view of the cameras with options to enable face blur, the number of tiles per page, camera name labels, and a camera auto rotate option.

How do I archive a video for later?

Archive video footage by clicking the Archive Icon on the camera’s History Player

What if I want to create an analytics dashboard that captures and displays specific data?

You can create a custom analytics dashboard to track the number of people or vehicles captured in specific cameras by going to the bar chart icon on the left hand side of the ‘cameras’ page.

How do I use the intercoms to communicate?

To use a Verkada intercom, access the Verkada Command platform, select the desired intercom device, and initiate a call by clicking the designated call button; the intercom will then attempt to reach the pre-configured receivers (like phones or other designated users) who can answer the call and view the live video feed all within the Command interface.

What if I want to search for specific things from the video feed like cars or people?

Verkada has AI powered search that allows users to search for people and vehicles using natural language, making it easier to find specific footage quickly.

My state doesn’t allow facial recognition. Should I turn this default off?

Yes, if you are in an area that does not allow facial recognition please turn this off

  • In Verkada Command, go to All Products > Cameras.
  • Select a camera.
  • Click Settings > Analytics.
  • Under People History > Face Detection toggle off Live Person of Interest Alerts.

Can I search through the audio recordings using the ai features?

Yes, to utilize the intercom for specific monitoring mentions follow these steps:

  • Access the Intercom: Click on the intercom button located on the left panel.
  • Navigate to Events: Select the 'Events' option and click on the sparkle icon.
  • Opt-In: Once you opt-in, click on the search button.
  • Search for Mentions: When you play a video, any relevant text chat should appear if the mentioned keywords were discussed.
  • AI Transcript Search: Type "AI transcript search" followed by the specific word you are looking for. The relevant mentions should be displayed.
  • Image Search: You can search across multiple images simultaneously.
  • Audio Search: Note that audio files must be searched individually.
